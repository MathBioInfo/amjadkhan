I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Dr. Rob Beiko's Bioinformatics group in the Faculty of Computer Science at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. I am developing mathematical models of antibiotic resistance propagation in bacteria, with a focus on certain genes that represent a high risk of transfer between clinical, agricultural, and environmental settings. I've a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Western University, Canada and M.Sc. in Mathematics from McMaster University, Canada.
