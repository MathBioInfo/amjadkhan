I am a Postdoctoral Fellow within Bioinformatics lab of Dr. Rob Beiko in the Faculty of Computer Science at Dalhousie University, Nova Scotia, Halifax, Canada. Here, I am developing mathematical models of the spread of antimicrobial resistance in bacteria, in particular relating to specific genes that pose very high risks of transmission between clinical, agricultural, and environmental settings.
